About

I'm a Madrid-born, Berlin-based graphic designer working across visual identity, creative direction, and art. My work is mainly rooted in music, culture, and electronic scenes, with a focus on branding, typography, poster systems, content design, and concept-driven visual languages. Alongside commissioned work, I also develop artistic and illustration-based projects.


I'm open for freelance inquiries, collaborations, and new projects. You can reach me at cokepereira008@gmail.com.
